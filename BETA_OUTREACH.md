Looking for 3 independent HubSpot admins to test an open-source form builder

### Post

I built **Form Builder - Typeform Alternative**, a free, open-source conversational form builder that sends mapped responses directly to HubSpot contacts.

The editor, hosted forms, iframe embedding, and contact sync are self-contained. It does not require Typeform, an automation service, or an external form-management API.

HubSpot requires three active installs from unaffiliated production accounts before a Marketplace listing can be submitted. I am looking for three HubSpot Super Admins from independent organizations to run a transparent five-minute production beta test:

1. Review the AGPL source, privacy policy, and requested contact read/write scopes.
2. Install through HubSpot OAuth.
3. Publish a small form and submit one contact you are authorized to add.
4. Verify the contact in HubSpot and keep the app installed through submission.

The checklist includes exact testing and cleanup steps. Disconnecting deletes the app connection, forms, and delivery logs; the test contact remains under your control in HubSpot.
